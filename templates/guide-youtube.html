Step-by-Step Guide to Download YouTube Videos

  1. Copy the YouTube URL. Open YouTube, navigate to any video or Short, and copy the URL from the address bar.
  2. Visit QuickSaveVideos. Go to QuickSaveVideos.com on any device.
  3. Paste and search. Paste the URL into the search box and click Search. The tool fetches the video details and available formats.
  4. Select your quality. Choose from Best, 1080p, 720p, 480p, 360p, or Audio MP3 for audio-only extraction.
