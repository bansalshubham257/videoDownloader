Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Copy the Facebook URL. Open Facebook, go to the video or Reel you want to download, and click the Share button then Copy Link. Alternatively, copy the URL from your browser's address bar.
  2. Visit QuickSaveVideos. Open QuickSaveVideos.com on any device.
  3. Paste and search. Paste the URL into the search box and click Search. The tool fetches the video and shows available quality options.
  4. Choose quality. Select Best, HD 720p, SD 480p, 360p, or Audio.
  5. Download. Click Download to save the video file to your device.

Troubleshooting & Tips

Video not loading?

Some Facebook videos are restricted by privacy settings. Only public videos from pages and profiles can be downloaded.

Can I download from Facebook groups?

Only if the group and the post are public. Private group content cannot be accessed.

Why is there no HD option?

Not all Facebook videos are uploaded in HD. The available quality depends on what was originally uploaded.
