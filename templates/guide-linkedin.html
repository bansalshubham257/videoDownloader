Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Copy the LinkedIn post URL. Open LinkedIn, go to the post with the video or image you want to download, and copy the URL from your browser's address bar. Alternatively, tap the three dots on the post and select Copy link to post.
  2. Visit QuickSaveVideos. Open QuickSaveVideos.com on any device.
  3. Paste and search. Paste the URL into the search box and click Search. The tool fetches the post media and shows a preview.
  4. Download. Click Download to save the video or image file to your device.

Supported LinkedIn Content

  • Post Videos — Videos shared in LinkedIn feed posts and articles.
  • Post Images — Photos and images shared in LinkedIn posts.
  • Native LinkedIn Videos — Videos uploaded directly to LinkedIn (not YouTube/Vimeo embeds).

Troubleshooting & Tips

Video not loading?

Some LinkedIn posts are restricted by privacy settings. Only public posts can be downloaded.

Can I download from LinkedIn profiles?

Only individual post URLs are supported. You need to open the specific post and copy its URL.

Why is there no HD option?

Not all LinkedIn videos are uploaded in HD. The available quality depends on what was originally uploaded.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I download LinkedIn videos on iPhone?

Yes, the site works on all browsers including Safari on iPhone. Downloads save to your Files app.

Only download content you own or have permission to use. Respect copyright and LinkedIn's terms.

No, QuickSaveVideos works for any public LinkedIn post without any paid subscription.

Can I download embedded YouTube videos from LinkedIn?

For embedded YouTube videos, paste the original YouTube URL directly into QuickSaveVideos for best results.
