Step-by-Step Guide to Download Bilibili Videos

  1. Find the Bilibili video. Open Bilibili and go to the video you want to download.
  2. Copy the URL. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar. It will look like bilibili.com/video/BVXXXXX or bilibili.tv/video/XXXXX.
  3. Visit QuickSaveVideos. Go to QuickSaveVideos.com.
  4. Paste and search. Paste the Bilibili URL into the search box and click Search. The tool fetches the video metadata and shows a preview with the thumbnail.
  5. Select quality and download. Choose your preferred quality: Best, 720p, 480p, 360p, or Audio MP3. Click Download to save the file.
