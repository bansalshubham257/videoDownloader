Why Use QuickSaveVideos for Twitter/X Downloads?

Twitter/X does not provide a direct download button for videos or high-resolution images. While you can save photos manually, videos require third-party tools. QuickSaveVideos makes it simple: paste a tweet URL and download all attached media instantly.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Copy the tweet URL. On Twitter/X, click the Share icon (three dots) on any tweet and select Copy Link. Alternatively, copy the full URL from your browser's address bar — it should contain /status/ followed by a number.
  2. Open QuickSaveVideos. Visit QuickSaveVideos.com.
  3. Paste and search. Paste the URL into the search box and click Search. The tool fetches all media attached to the tweet.
  4. Download each item. Photos and videos appear in a grid. Click Download on each item to save it to your device.
💡 Tip: Tweets with multiple images show each photo separately. You can download individual photos without downloading the entire set.

Troubleshooting

Tweet not loading?

Ensure the tweet is from a public account. Protected tweets cannot be accessed. Also verify the URL contains /status/.

Video quality seems low?

Twitter/X compresses uploaded videos. The download matches the quality available on the platform.

Can I download from X Premium?

Yes, long-form videos posted by X Premium subscribers are also supported.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I download multiple tweets at once?

Currently QuickSaveVideos processes one tweet at a time. Paste each tweet URL individually.

Are deleted tweets downloadable?

No, once a tweet is deleted or the account is suspended, the media is no longer accessible.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes, the site works on all mobile browsers including Safari and Chrome on iOS and Android.
