Step-by-Step Guide to Download Pinterest Videos

  1. Copy the pin URL. Open Pinterest, navigate to the pin you want to download, and copy the URL from your browser's address bar. Pinterest URLs follow the format pinterest.com/pin/1234567890/.
  2. Visit QuickSaveVideos. Go to QuickSaveVideos.com.
  3. Paste and search. Paste the pin URL into the search box and click Search. The tool fetches the pin and displays a preview.
  4. Download. If the pin contains a video, click Download to save the MP4. For image pins, the image is shown and ready to save.

Supported Pinterest Content

Troubleshooting

Pin not loading?

Only public pins are accessible. Secret boards and private pins cannot be downloaded.

Video not playing after download?

Some Pinterest videos use non-standard codecs. Try playing the file in VLC Media Player which supports more formats.

Can I download entire boards?

Currently QuickSaveVideos processes one pin at a time. Paste each pin URL individually.

Frequently Asked Questions

What resolution are downloaded images?

Images are downloaded at the original resolution uploaded to Pinterest, which is typically the highest quality version.

Can I download Pinterest videos on iPhone?

Yes, the site works on Safari and Chrome for iOS. Videos download to your Files app.

Is it legal to download Pinterest content?

Only download pins you own or have permission to use. Respect the creator's rights and Pinterest's terms of service.

Do you support video downloads from Pinterest?

Yes, video pins are fully supported. The tool detects whether the pin contains a video or image and offers the appropriate download option.
